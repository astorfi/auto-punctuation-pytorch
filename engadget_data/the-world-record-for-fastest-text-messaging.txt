

Setting a world speed record for the one skill that we hope will become obsolete in the very near future, James
Trusler has emerged as the world's fastest text messager, tapping out, "The razor-toothed piranhas of the genera
Serrasalmus and Pygocentrus are the most ferocious freshwater fish in the world. In reality they seldom attack a human"
on his cellphone in a mere 67 seconds, shaving nearly a minute off of the previous record.

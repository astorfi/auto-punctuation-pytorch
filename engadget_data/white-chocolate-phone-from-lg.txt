
   
LG is throwing down a"White Chocolate" version of their SV590 Chocolate phone that they pumped out for Valentines day. It's adecently specced slider, with 512MB of included memory, MP3 playback, and a 2-inch QVGA screen all squeezed into0.6-inches of thickness. The real draw here, of course, is fashion, but we can't say it'd suck for musicphone duties.

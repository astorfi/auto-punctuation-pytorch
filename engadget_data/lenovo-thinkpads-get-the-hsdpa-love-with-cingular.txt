
       Lenovo, the newmaster of the ThinkPad, already cut a dealwith Verizon Wireless to offer laptops that are compatible with the carrier's high-speed EV-DO wireless network,now they've just announced that they're going to be offering ThinkPads that'll work with Cingular's new HSDPA network(Sony already offers a Vaio that works with Cingular's EDGE network) as well. No word yet on pricing or which lappieswill be compatible, but we'll post up details as soon as we have them.

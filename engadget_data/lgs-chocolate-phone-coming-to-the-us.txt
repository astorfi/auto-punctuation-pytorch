
   
We've had our eye on LG's"Chocolate" 0.6-inch thick slider for a little while now. The phone sports a2-inch QVGA screen, 512MB of memory, and is selling like crazy in Korea. Well now it's headed to our shores, thoughthere's no word on which carrier will pick it up. LG has both CDMA and GSM versions available, so it's anybody's game.The phone should arrive sometime this year, while a GSM version should hit Western Europe this May.

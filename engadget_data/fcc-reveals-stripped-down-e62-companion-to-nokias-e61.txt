
   
Either as a bid to further compete with the nowfree and clear Blackberry, ormerely as penance for their historical lack of QWERTY phones, it seems like Nokia is taking their e61 form factor to the low-end with a new e62 phone that hassurfaced on the FCC site. The e62 loses the 3G and WiFi of its big brother, along with some other slight modificationsincluding the apparent lack of push email connectivity. But otherwise this phone should serve well those who want theiremail anywhere, but have are willing to wait a bit longer to receive it.[Via Phone Scoop]

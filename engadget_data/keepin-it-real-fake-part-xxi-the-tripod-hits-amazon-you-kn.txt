
 
We're accustomed to seeing fake iPods making the rounds overseas, and a dodgy Craigslist deal doesn't seem out of the question, but we're quite impressed with the 3rd party vendor that managed to get this TRiPod ripoff of the iPod nano onto the Amazon.com US site. It seems to be fairly similar to the 2GB nano rip that made it to Craigslist last month, with video playing abilities and similar software, but this one is 1GB and goes for $185 -- at least until Stevie J. gets Jeff Bezos on the horn for a little chit chat.

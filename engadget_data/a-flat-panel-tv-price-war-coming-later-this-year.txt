

You may want to wait a few months before buying that new jumbo-sized flat-panel TV. Reuters reports that while
prices for smaller TVs are probably going to go up later this year, that Sony, LG, Panasonic, Samsung, Pioneer, and
other manufacturers are getting ready for a price war and are expected to drop prices on LCD and plasma TVs that are
larger than 40-inches in size in order to grab more market share. We can't wait.

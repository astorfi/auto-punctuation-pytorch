
   
All the DMB-capable PMPs start to bleed together after a while, andthis 3550 unit from CLIOD seems no exception. It does the usual MPEG, DivX, Windows Media 9, and supports an externalantenna to nab some DMB television. The 3550 sports either a 20GB or 30GB HDD and goes for around $450. Seems a littlesteep for a device with a 3-inch screen, but who are we to judge?

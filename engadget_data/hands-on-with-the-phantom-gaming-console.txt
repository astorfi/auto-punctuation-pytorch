

We almost don't want to believe it, but Gamers.com got a hands-on with the Phantom, that PC gaming console from
Infinium Labs which has been so shrouded in disinformation, false starts, and premature product announcements that a
lot of people are convinced it's never going to come out. The tried out Unreal Tournament 2004 and said they
couldn't tell the difference between playing in on the Phantom and playing it on a regular PC.

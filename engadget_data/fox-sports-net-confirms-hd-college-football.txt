
 We told you there were rumors that FSN would present a few College football games in our favorite format this year and Brodcasting&Cable is reporting that it's true.They will now offer national HD games in addition to their local and regional HDTV, 15 of their 23 national games of Big 12 and Pac-10 college football games will be 720p. The games will be available on FSN HD, which is available on DirecTV as well as many cable companies around the country. The first game is Utah at UCLA on Saturday, September 2 at 7:00 p.m EST.

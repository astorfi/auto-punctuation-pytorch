
   
We've been hearingrumblings of a Sharp VGA phonesince before 3GSM, butVodafone has finally announced the 904SH, a 3G phone sporting four times the fun of QVGA at a 480x640 resolution.There's also a 3.2 megapixel camera with 2x optical zoom and autofocus, a 3D motion sensor, Bluetooth, GPS, miniSDstorage, and WCDMA (UMTS) data. The phone should be available in Japan late April, let's hope for some swiftimports.[Warning: PDF link!][Via PhoneScoop]

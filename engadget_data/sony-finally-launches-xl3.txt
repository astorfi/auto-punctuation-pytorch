
 
Sony launched its XL2 media PC about this time last year, but the XL3 finally brings to the table the high def experience, by way of a built-in ATSC (and NTSC) tuner, CableCARD (i.e. DCT), and uses Core 2 Duo processors, NVIDIA GeForce 7600 GTL graphics, 2GB of RAM, and a surprisingly thin 500GB hard drive. Yours March for $3,300. (Yeah, Sony's XL3 site is down at the time of this writing, so we had to show you the XL2 to refresh your memory.)

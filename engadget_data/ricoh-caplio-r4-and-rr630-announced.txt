
 
As usual, Ricoh's digital cameras are aimed a slightly more particularaudience than your general compact digital cameras, but anyone interested in a machine with a 6 megapixel sensor, 7.1optical zoom (seriously), 2.5-inch display, and optical image stabilization should take a look at Ricoh's new£249 ($435 US) Caplio R4; anyone looking for something slightly more budgetastic should peep Ricoh's £129($261 US) RR630, which itself has a 6 megapixel sensor, 3x zoom, 2-inch screen, and takes AA cells.[Via Digicam Review]

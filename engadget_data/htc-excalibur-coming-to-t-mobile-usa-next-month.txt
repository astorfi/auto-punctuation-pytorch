
       
We've gotten plenty of face time with the HTC Excalibur at CTIA this week, but even though we've known about this one for a while now, what we hadn't known for certain was when it was going to actually arrive here in the States. Well, according to one of our most reliable sources, T-Mobile will be offering the phone beginning sometime next month. He couldn't confirm for us whether or not it'll be called the "T-Mobile Dash" or what it might be priced at, but as soon as we know for sure we'll let you know.
